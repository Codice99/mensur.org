This Privacy Notice explains how personal data submitted through the Mensur contact form is processed.

## Controller

Mensur | A Weissmann–Greiffenberg Institute Project  
Contact: **contact@mensur.org**

At this stage, Mensur is an emerging cultural and intellectual project connected with Gabriel Gabrielli Sanchez.

## Data collected

When you use the contact form, the following data may be collected:

- name;
- email address;
- phone number, if voluntarily provided;
- message content;
- technical metadata necessary for delivery and spam prevention.

## Purpose

The data is used exclusively to:

- receive and manage enquiries;
- respond to messages;
- handle scholarly, cultural, institutional or archival correspondence;
- prevent spam or abusive use of the contact form.

## Legal basis

The legal basis is the consent given by the user when submitting the form and the legitimate interest in managing correspondence addressed to Mensur.

## Recipients and processors

Messages submitted through the contact form may be processed by technical service providers used for form delivery, domain/email forwarding and email reception.

Personal data will not be sold, published or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Retention

Messages will be kept only for as long as necessary to manage the enquiry, maintain a reasonable correspondence record, or comply with applicable legal obligations.

## Rights

Under European data protection law, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection or portability where applicable.

You may exercise these rights by writing to:

**contact@mensur.org**

## Cookies and analytics

Mensur may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand general website traffic and performance, including page views, visitor counts, referrers, device types and general location information.

Cloudflare Web Analytics is intended to provide aggregated website metrics without using analytics cookies or tracking individual users across websites.

At this stage, Mensur does not use advertising cookies, behavioural tracking cookies or Google Analytics.

If non-essential cookies or additional tracking technologies are introduced in the future, the website should provide appropriate information and, where required, obtain consent.
