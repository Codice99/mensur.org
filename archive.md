<div class="cripta-panel">
  <p>
    The Cripta is conceived as a restricted documentary chamber for authorised members, designated collaborators and approved researchers connected with the Weissmann-Greiffenberg Institute Project.
  </p>

  <p>
    Access to internal genealogical material, family documentary references, private archival notes, images, working dossiers and protected research files is not public.
  </p>

  <p>
    The future private archive will be reserved for authorised access only. Sensitive personal documents, identity documents, private signatures, addresses, emails and data concerning living persons are not published on the public website.
  </p>
</div>
