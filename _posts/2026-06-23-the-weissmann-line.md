The Weissmann line belongs to the Germanic–Alpine research field of the Mensur project.

This section will collect documentary material, family memory, hypotheses and historical notes concerning the Weissmann family line, its Germanic context and its possible northern and eastern connections.

The line will be studied carefully, distinguishing between documentary evidence, oral family memory, symbolic interpretation and open hypotheses.
