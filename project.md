Mensur is an independent research archive dedicated to the study of lineage, European memory, legal structures, symbolism and intellectual history.

The project is structured around the Weissmann and Greiffenberg lines, but it is not limited to genealogy. It also includes doctoral research, legal analysis, cultural history, languages, archival work and symbolic interpretation.

Mensur is not a commercial platform. It is a digital archive, research notebook and long-form essay space.

## Purpose

The purpose of Mensur is to preserve, organise and develop research across several connected fields:

- family memory and genealogy;
- European legal and historical structures;
- Central European, Mediterranean, Nordic and Eastern European research lines;
- doctoral work on European energy law and hydrogen regulation;
- symbolism, heraldry and intellectual history;
- essays, notes and archival fragments.
